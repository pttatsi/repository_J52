Introspective Glances | A Look At Ana Mendieta




I learned of Ana Mendieta after the passing of her abusive husband, Carl Andre, January 24, 2024. Since then I have been mersmerized by her work with exploring the human body and psyche, our connection to earth and the spiritual connections that surround us. Mendieta is an undoubtable auteur, recognized for her performance art and avant-garde nature.
