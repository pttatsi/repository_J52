Why you should Support your local Thrift

As a student in College Park who loves and adores the act of thrifting, it is important to support local thrift shops and stores that often support charitable organizations, with proceeds from sales helping fun community projects or social services. While Value Village, College Park is a for-profit thrift store, they buy donated clothing from non-profit organizations that fund programs such as after-school activities for children in our communities.
